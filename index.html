Welcome In Abha.

Located at the southern end of the Red Sea coast, Abha is the culturally rich capital of the Asir region and a great base for exploring this mountainous part of Arabia. The city has a wealth of delights to discover, from the mud-walled Shada Palace, now a museum, to Abha Dam Lake and the Abha Palace Theme Park.
